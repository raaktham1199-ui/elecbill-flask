⚡Check the electricity bill for electrical appliances

🔍 How to check

  • Specify the wattage (W) or power rating of the appliance you want to calculate.
  • Enter the daily usage hours correctly for accurate results
  • Select the month and year to check the bill.
  • Check the electricity rate per unit to ensure it is up to date.
  • Check that fields aren't empty or unrealistic.

ℹ️ Note

  • If you don't know the wattage, check the energy label or sticker on the device.
  • Unrealistic values may distort results.

⚠️ Power-usage warnings

  • Using low-efficiency or old appliances.
  • Damaged or loose cables/plugs can cause unstable current.
  • Dust buildup in ACs, fridges, or fans makes the motor work harder.
  • Leaving appliances on.
  • An overfilled fridge makes the cooling system work harder.
  • Charging many devices on one power strip can cause overload.
